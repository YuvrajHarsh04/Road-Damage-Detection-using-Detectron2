About Road Damage Detection

Our Road Damage Detection project leverages machine learning and image recognition to detect and classify road damages such as cracks, potholes, and other structural defects. This system helps in quick identification of areas needing repair, ensuring better road maintenance and safety.

By using cutting-edge AI algorithms, our model processes real-time data to pinpoint damage with high accuracy, assisting road maintenance authorities to prioritize their efforts.
